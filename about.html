ABOUT US

As the world today is witnessing great increase in exploration, so is the number of people going down daily with mental disorder as a result of stress, family or relationship problems, terrorism or life threatening illness is fast increasing more than one can ever imagine. A mental disorder can make you miserable and can cause problems in your daily life such as school, work or relationships. In this 21st century, especially in Africa (Nigeria), people tend to care less about their mental health while some are not even aware they are mentally ill and that is why At Peace Centre Limited has taken out time to bring to your door step the ‘Talking Cure’ as the solution to the problem of “The 21st Century”. At Peace Centre Limited (APC) is a psychotherapy centre which was incorporated in October, 2015. With our experts in the field of clinical psychology, psychology and Counseling, we provide our clients with the best service, exceeding their expectation. We have the answer to the problems that have been troubling your mind. Don’t bear it alone, we are always there for you to lean on. Our Services are tailored to fit each individual, group or company.

Vision

To give everyone a chance to live a fulfilled life, in this ever changing and challenging world. To alleviate pains, promote total well-being, deliver exceptional services and exceed the expectations of our stakeholders.

Mission

To inspire hope and contribute to health and well-being, to resolve, mitigate, improve relationships and social functioning by identifying and changing dysfunctional thinking, behavior and emotional responses.

Our Value Statement

  1. Integrity: - Adhering to the highest standard of professionalism, ethics and personal responsibility, worthy of the trust our clients placed in us.
  2. Respect: - Treat everyone in our diverse community including patients, their families and colleagues with dignity.
  3. Compassion: - Providing the best care, treating patients and family members with sensitivity and love.
  4. Healing: - inspire hope and nurture the wellbeing of the whole person, respecting physical, emotional and psychological needs.
  5. Team work: - value the contributions of all, blending the skills of individual staff members in an unsurpassed collaboration.
  6. Excellence: - deliver the best outcome and highest quality services through the dedicated effort of every team member.
  7. Customer’s Satisfaction: - Engaging in multimodal programs and tools to create a fresh and personal wellness experience that is transformational.
